A layered cascade tucked in Cherrapunji's mist. The bamboo stair trek is an adventure itself — once you reach the base, a serene pool awaits. Best visited Oct–Apr for clear skies, or monsoon for dramatic falls.
Spotted in reels
Best season Oct–Apr for clear rivers and road trips; monsoon for waterfalls and greenery.
